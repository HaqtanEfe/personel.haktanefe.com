About me

the long version

Name's Haktan, but you'll see me as Haq or HaqtanEfe in most places. 17, from Turkey, half-glued to a keyboard since I was 9.

I started by breaking computers I wasn't supposed to be touching. Then I figured out you could fix them, then make them do things, and that was that.

I script Roblox for work, but most of my time goes to side quests — Linux distros, self-hosted servers that may or may not survive the week, Java modding, flight physics for a plane game, weird Python scripts that solved problems nobody had.

Night owl, introvert with social battery, try to learn one new thing a week. I love the moment something just works after I've been fighting it for hours.
